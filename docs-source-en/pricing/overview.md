## Fee overview

Waffo adopts a transparent pricing strategy. All fees are fully disclosed before contract signing, with no hidden charges. Key fee components include:

**Acquiring fees:**
- International credit/debit cards: percentage of transaction amount + a fixed fee (Interchange++ or blended pricing)
- Local payment methods: priced separately by specific channel
- 3DS authentication: some channels charge an additional 3DS processing fee

**Payout fees:**
- Fixed fee per payout, tiered by destination region and channel type
- Batch Payouts benefit from volume discounts

**Other fees:**
- FX conversion: FX spread, no additional fee
- Dispute handling: a processing fee is charged per Chargeback (refunded if you win)
- Refunds: no additional fee; original transaction fees are not refunded

Specific rates vary by the merchant’s region, business type, and monthly processing volume. Please contact the Waffo sales team for a tailored quote.

## Billing models

Waffo offers two primary billing models. Merchants can choose based on their business needs:

**Blended pricing:**
- A single unified rate for all transactions, simple and transparent billing
- Suitable for merchants with lower volume or a single payment method
- Example rate: offshore credit card X% + $Y per transaction

**IC++ pass-through pricing (Interchange-plus-plus):**
- Billed as actual Interchange rates + a fixed Waffo markup
- High-volume merchants can achieve a lower effective overall rate
- Transparent statements: each transaction clearly shows the Interchange fee, scheme fee, and Waffo markup
- Suitable for mature merchants whose monthly processing volume exceeds a certain threshold

## FAQ

**Q: Are there monthly subscription fees or platform fees?**  
A: Waffo’s standard plan has no monthly subscription fee and is charged only based on transaction volume. Some advanced features (such as a white-label checkout and custom domain) may incur additional charges.

**Q: Are fees refunded when a refund is issued?**  
A: The refund itself does not incur an additional fee, but the payment processing fees from the original transaction are not refunded.

**Q: Are FX rates real-time?**  
A: Yes. Waffo uses real-time FX rates and locks the rate at the time of payment. The merchant settlement amount is calculated based on the locked rate.

**Q: How can I view historical invoices?**  
A: The Settlement Management module in the Merchant Portal provides a complete invoice history and supports downloading detailed monthly statements.
