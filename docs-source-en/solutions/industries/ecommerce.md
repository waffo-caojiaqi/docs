## Industry overview

Cross-border ecommerce is one of Waffo’s core service areas. As global consumer demand for overseas shopping continues to grow, cross-border ecommerce platforms and independent merchants must address complex challenges such as multi-currency Acquiring, cross-border Settlement, and localized payment experiences. Waffo is deeply focused on cross-border ecommerce payment scenarios, providing merchants with a complete fund flow solution from customer payment to merchant collection.

## Core needs

The primary payment needs of cross-border ecommerce merchants include:

- **Localized Acquiring across multiple markets**: collect payments in local currencies and via local payment methods in different countries/regions to improve conversion rates
- **Multi-currency Settlement**: flexibly choose the Settlement currency to reduce FX losses
- **Seller Payouts**: platform ecommerce needs to quickly pay out to sellers worldwide
- **Refund processing**: support cross-border refunds to protect the customer experience
- **Chargeback and fraud prevention**: effectively identify and block fraudulent transactions and control Chargeback rates

## Waffo solution

Waffo provides an end-to-end payment solution for cross-border ecommerce:

- **Global Acquiring**: support Acquiring in 50+ countries and 30+ currencies, covering cards and mainstream local payment methods
- **Smart routing**: automatically select the optimal payment Channel to maximize payment success rates
- **Seller Payouts**: make batch payouts to global sellers via Waffo Payout, supporting local bank transfers
- **FX management**: transparent exchange rates, support for multi-currency accounts, and flexible management of FX exposure
- **One-stop Reconciliation**: a unified reporting platform with automated Reconciliation to reduce finance workload
