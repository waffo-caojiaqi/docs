## 中文版

### Waffo 产品全景

构建全球支付基础设施，从收单到增长的全链路解决方案。

---

### 1. 收单支付 (Acquiring & Payments)

连接全球支付网络，提供灵活多样的收款方式，满足不同业务形态的交易需求。

#### 1.1 一次性支付 (One-time Payment)

标准的即时交易模式（Sale），支持全球主流信用卡与本地支付方式。

- **特点**：极速获批，实时反馈支付结果。
- **价值**：适合电商零售、数字游戏等"一手交钱一手交货"的场景，保障现金流快速回笼。

#### 1.2 订阅支付与自动扣款 (Subscription & Recurring)

专为 SaaS 和会员制业务打造的周期性扣款引擎。

- **核心能力**：
  - **智能重试 (Smart Retry)**：基于大数据分析最佳重试时间，挽回因余额不足导致的失败。
  - **被动流失管理**：有效降低因卡片问题导致的非自愿流失 (Involuntary Churn)。
- **适用场景**：软件订阅、会员续费、定期购。

#### 1.3 收银台 (Hosted Checkout)

开箱即用的托管式支付页面，无需开发前端代码即可上线。

- **核心能力**：
  - **多端适配**：自动适应 PC、移动端与 Pad 屏幕。
  - **品牌定制**：支持上传 Logo、自定义配色，保持品牌一致性。
  - **动态路由**：根据用户所在地自动展示最常用的本地支付方式。

#### 1.4 支付方式 (Payment Methods)

一站式接入全球主流支付渠道。

- **覆盖范围**：国际卡组织（Visa, Mastercard, Amex）及 100+ 本地钱包（如 Apple Pay, Google Pay, Alipay 等）。

---

### 2. 结算与资金管理 (Settlement & Funds)

透明、合规的全球资金与税务管理体系，消除跨境经营的后顾之忧。

#### 2.1 商户结算 (Merchant Settlement)

灵活的全球资金分发网络。

- **核心能力**：
  - **多币种归集**：支持原币种结算或自动换汇结算，降低汇损。
  - **灵活周期**：支持从 T+1 极速结算到月结的多种账期选择。

#### 2.2 全球税务合规 (Global Tax / MoR)

基于 Merchant of Record (MoR) 模式的自动化税务托管服务。

- **核心能力**：
  - **自动计税与申报**：实时计算全球 100+ 国家的 VAT/GST，由 Waffo 负责向税务局申报并缴纳。
  - **B2B/B2C 智能区分**：支持反向征收 (Reverse Charge) 机制，自动免除合规企业的税款。
- **价值**：无需在海外注册实体即可合规展业，彻底规避税务风险。

---

### 3. 增值服务 (Value-Added Services)

超越支付本身，通过数据洞察与风险管理赋能业务增长。

#### 3.1 数据分析 (Data Intelligence)

全视角的支付健康度监控与诊断中心。

- **核心能力**：
  - **全景看板**：秒级监控 Auth Rate（授权成功率）、TPV 及全渠道转化漏斗。
  - **智能归因**：将晦涩的银行拒绝码转化为可执行策略（区分技术故障、风控拦截或用户流失）。
  - **流失分析**：量化挽回收益，精准定位订阅流失根源。

#### 3.2 商户 Portal 与争议管理

一站式商户管理平台，涵盖交易查询、结算管理、拒付处理与账户配置。

---

### 4. 高级功能 (Advanced Features)

#### 4.1 Tokenization (凭证化)

将持卡人敏感信息替换为安全 Token，支持后续免密支付（MIT）与订阅扣款。

#### 4.2 Auth & Capture (预授权与请款)

先冻结资金后请款的两阶段支付流程，适合酒店预订、线下核销等场景。

## English Version

### Waffo Product Overview

Building global payment infrastructure — an end-to-end solution from acquiring to growth.

---

### 1. Acquiring & Payments

Connect to the global payment network with flexible, diverse collection methods to meet transaction needs across different business models.

#### 1.1 One-time Payment

Standard instant transaction (Sale) supporting global mainstream credit cards and local payment methods.

- **Feature**: Ultra-fast approval with real-time payment result feedback.
- **Value**: Ideal for e-commerce retail, digital games, and other "pay now, deliver now" scenarios, ensuring rapid cash flow recovery.

#### 1.2 Subscription & Recurring Payment

A periodic billing engine built specifically for SaaS and membership-based businesses.

- **Core capabilities**:
  - **Smart Retry**: Analyzes optimal retry times using big data to recover failed payments due to insufficient funds.
  - **Passive Churn Management**: Effectively reduces involuntary churn caused by card issues.
- **Use cases**: Software subscriptions, membership renewals, regular purchases.

#### 1.3 Hosted Checkout

An out-of-the-box hosted payment page — go live without developing frontend code.

- **Core capabilities**:
  - **Multi-device adaptation**: Automatically adapts to PC, mobile, and tablet screens.
  - **Brand customization**: Upload logo, customize color schemes to maintain brand consistency.
  - **Dynamic routing**: Automatically displays the most-used local payment methods based on user location.

#### 1.4 Payment Methods

One-stop access to global mainstream payment channels.

- **Coverage**: International card networks (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) and 100+ local wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay, Alipay, etc.).

---

### 2. Settlement & Fund Management

Transparent, compliant global fund and tax management, eliminating cross-border business concerns.

#### 2.1 Merchant Settlement

A flexible global fund distribution network.

- **Core capabilities**:
  - **Multi-currency aggregation**: Supports original currency settlement or automatic FX settlement to reduce exchange losses.
  - **Flexible cycles**: Supports multiple payment cycles from T+1 express settlement to monthly settlement.

#### 2.2 Global Tax (MoR)

Automated tax custody service based on the Merchant of Record (MoR) model.

- **Core capabilities**:
  - **Automatic tax calculation and filing**: Real-time calculation of VAT/GST for 100+ countries globally, with Waffo responsible for filing and payment to tax authorities.
  - **B2B/B2C intelligent differentiation**: Supports Reverse Charge mechanism, automatically exempting compliant businesses from tax.
- **Value**: Operate compliantly globally without registering overseas entities, completely eliminating tax risks.

---

### 3. Value-Added Services

Beyond payments — empowering business growth through data insights and risk management.

#### 3.1 Data Analysis

A comprehensive payment health monitoring and diagnostic center.

- **Core capabilities**:
  - **Panoramic dashboard**: Real-time monitoring of Auth Rate, TPV, and full-channel conversion funnel.
  - **Intelligent attribution**: Translates cryptic bank decline codes into actionable strategies (distinguishing technical failures, risk control blocks, or user churn).
  - **Churn analysis**: Quantify recovery gains and precisely identify subscription churn root causes.

#### 3.2 Merchant Portal & Dispute Management

An all-in-one merchant management platform covering transaction queries, settlement management, chargeback handling, and account configuration.

---

### 4. Advanced Features

#### 4.1 Tokenization

Replace cardholder sensitive information with secure tokens, supporting subsequent password-free payments (MIT) and subscription billing.

#### 4.2 Auth & Capture

A two-phase payment process that freezes funds first then captures — suitable for hotel reservations, offline verification, and similar scenarios.
