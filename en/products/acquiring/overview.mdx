## Chinese version

### Waffo product landscape

Building global payment infrastructure—an end-to-end solution from acquiring to growth.

---

### 1. Acquiring & payments (Acquiring & Payments)

Connect to global payment networks and provide flexible, diverse payment collection methods to meet transaction needs across different business models.

#### 1.1 One-time payment (One-time Payment)

A standard real-time transaction model (Sale) that supports major global credit cards and local payment methods.

- **Features**: Fast approvals and real-time payment result feedback.
- **Value**: Ideal for e-commerce retail, digital games, and other “pay and deliver” scenarios—helping ensure rapid cash collection.

#### 1.2 Subscription payments and recurring billing (Subscription & Recurring)

A recurring billing engine built specifically for SaaS and membership businesses.

- **Core capabilities**:
  - **Smart retry (Smart Retry)**: Uses big data to determine the best retry timing to recover failures caused by insufficient funds.
  - **Passive churn management**: Effectively reduces involuntary churn caused by card issues (Involuntary Churn).
- **Use cases**: Software subscriptions, membership renewals, and scheduled purchases.

#### 1.3 Hosted checkout (Hosted Checkout)

An out-of-the-box hosted payment page that can go live without front-end development.

- **Core capabilities**:
  - **Multi-device responsiveness**: Automatically adapts to PC, mobile, and tablet screens.
  - **Brand customization**: Supports logo upload and custom color themes to maintain brand consistency.
  - **Dynamic routing**: Automatically shows the most commonly used local payment methods based on the user’s location.

#### 1.4 Payment methods (Payment Methods)

One-stop access to major global payment channels.

- **Coverage**: International card schemes (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) and 100+ local wallets (e.g., Apple Pay, Google Pay, Alipay, etc.).

---

### 2. Settlement & funds (Settlement & Funds)

A transparent, compliant global funds and tax management system to remove cross-border operating concerns.

#### 2.1 Merchant settlement (Merchant Settlement)

A flexible global funds distribution network.

- **Core capabilities**：
  - **Multi-currency collection**: Supports settlement in the original currency or automatic FX conversion settlement to reduce FX loss.
  - **Flexible cycles**: Supports multiple settlement schedules, from T+1 fast settlement to monthly settlement.

#### 2.2 Global tax compliance (Global Tax / MoR)

An automated tax hosting service based on the Merchant of Record (MoR) model.

- **Core capabilities**：
  - **Automated tax calculation and filing**: Real-time calculation of VAT/GST across 100+ countries, with Waffo responsible for filing and remitting to tax authorities.
  - **Intelligent B2B/B2C differentiation**: Supports the reverse charge (Reverse Charge) mechanism, automatically exempting compliant businesses from tax.
- **Value**: Operate compliantly without registering local entities overseas, fully mitigating tax risk.

---

### 3. Value-added services (Value-Added Services)

Go beyond payments to enable business growth through data insights and risk control.

#### 3.1 Data intelligence (Data Intelligence)

A full-view payment health monitoring and diagnostics center.

- **Core capabilities**：
  - **Panoramic dashboards**: Second-level monitoring of Auth Rate (authorization success rate), TPV, and full-channel conversion funnels.
  - **Intelligent attribution**: Converts obscure bank decline codes into actionable strategies (distinguishing technical failures, risk control blocks, or user churn).
  - **Churn analysis**: Quantifies recovery uplift and precisely identifies the root causes of subscription churn.

#### 3.2 Merchant portal and dispute management

A one-stop merchant management platform covering transaction search, settlement management, chargeback handling, and account configuration.

---

### 4. Advanced features (Advanced Features)

#### 4.1 Tokenization

Replace sensitive cardholder data with secure tokens to support subsequent card-on-file payments (MIT) and subscription billing.

#### 4.2 Auth & capture

A two-step payment flow that authorizes (reserves) funds first and captures later—ideal for hotel bookings, offline fulfillment/verification, and similar scenarios.
