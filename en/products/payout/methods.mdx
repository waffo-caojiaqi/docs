## Supported payout methods

Waffo Payout supports multiple disbursement channels. Merchants can choose flexibly based on the destination country, timeline requirements, and cost preferences.

### Bank transfer

#### Local fast transfer (Local Transfer)

Funds are sent through the local clearing network in the destination country, typically credited on T+0 or T+1. Fees are usually lower than SWIFT international wire transfers.

| Region | Clearing network | Funds availability |
|------|----------|----------|
| China | CNAPS (HVPS/LVPS) | Real-time ~ T+1 |
| United States | ACH / Fedwire | T+1 ~ T+3 |
| Europe | SEPA SCT | T+0 ~ T+1 |
| United Kingdom | Faster Payments | Real-time |
| India | IMPS / NEFT | Real-time ~ T+1 |
| Singapore | FAST | Real-time |
| Australia | NPP / OSKO | Real-time |
| Thailand | PromptPay | Real-time |

#### SWIFT international wire transfer

For countries not covered by local transfers, cross-border remittances are sent via the SWIFT interbank network.

- **Funds availability**: T+2 ~ T+5 business days
- **Coverage**: 200+ countries and regions
- **Use cases**: Large cross-border payments, supplier payments

### Card refund (Card Refund)

Funds are returned to the original payment card, suitable for refund scenarios.

- **Funds availability**: 3 ~ 10 business days (depending on the issuer’s processing speed)
- **Characteristics**: Funds are refunded directly to the cardholder’s account for a smooth user experience
- **Limitations**: The amount must not exceed the original transaction amount; some card schemes limit the initiation time window

### Instant push to card (Push to Card)

Funds are pushed instantly to the cardholder’s debit card account via Visa Direct or Mastercard Send.

- **Funds availability**: Typically within 30 minutes
- **Supported card networks**: Visa, Mastercard
- **Use cases**: Insurance claims, incentive payouts, marketplace seller earnings

## Funds availability timeline

| Payout method | Fastest | Standard timeline | Notes |
|-------------|----------|----------|------|
| Real-time local transfer | Instant | < 1 minute | Limited to supported countries |
| Local ACH/SEPA | T+0 | T+1 | Processed on business days |
| SWIFT wire transfer | T+2 | T+3~5 | Intermediary banks may charge additional fees |
| Digital wallets | Instant | < 5 minutes | Wallet account must be activated |
| Card refund | T+3 | T+5~10 | Depends on issuer processing speed |
| Push to Card | < 30 minutes | < 2 hours | Requires Visa Direct support |

<Info>
Funds availability is affected by the beneficiary bank’s processing speed, holidays, FX clearing, and other factors. The above timelines are for reference only and are not guaranteed.
</Info>

## Fee details

Waffo Payout uses transparent pricing. Merchants can view the estimated fee for each Payout in the dashboard.

### Fee components

| Fee type | Description |
|----------|------|
| Payout service fee | Processing fee charged by Waffo, billed per transaction or as a percentage of amount |
| Intermediary bank fee | Intermediary bank fees that may occur in SWIFT cross-border wires (charged by third parties) |
| FX conversion fee | When currency conversion is involved, calculated as a markup over the real-time exchange rate |
| Refund | For failed Payouts, the service fee will be refunded in the next settlement cycle |

### Rate range reference

| Payout method | Rate range |
|-------------|----------|
| Local bank transfer | 0.3% ~ 0.8% (with minimum fee) |
| SWIFT international wire transfer | Fixed fee + 0.5% ~ 1.5% |
| Digital wallets | 0.2% ~ 0.6% |
| Card refund | Original transaction fee is non-refundable + refund processing fee |

<Note>
Actual rates depend on the merchant’s contract terms, Payout volume, and destination country. Contact the Waffo business team for a dedicated quote.
</Note>
