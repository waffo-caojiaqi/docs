## Service introduction

Waffo Payout (Payout) is a fund disbursement service designed for platform merchants, enabling merchants to send funds quickly and securely to recipients worldwide. Whether issuing refunds to users, paying suppliers, or sharing revenue with platform sellers, Waffo Payout provides a unified operating interface and API.

The Payout service supports both single payouts and batch processing, covering multiple fund flow paths such as bank transfers, digital wallets, and card refunds, and provides end-to-end settlement status tracking and reconciliation reports.

## Applicable scenarios

### Platform seller revenue sharing

For multi-sided marketplaces such as e-commerce platforms and content platforms, periodically settling earnings to sellers and creators. Waffo Payout supports batch disbursements by settlement cycle, automatically handling currency conversion and fee deductions.

### Supplier payment settlement

In cross-border trade scenarios, paying overseas suppliers for goods. Supports both SWIFT wire transfers and local fast transfers, allowing flexible selection based on timeliness and cost.

### User refunds and reward payouts

For refund processing and user rewards (cashback, points redemption), Payout provides a more flexible disbursement method than original-route refunds, supporting delivery to an account different from the one used for payment.

### Employee payroll payouts

Payroll disbursements for multinational teams, supporting multi-currency settlement and automatically converting payroll amounts at the daily exchange rate before sending to designated bank accounts.

### Insurance claims

Insurers can quickly pay claim amounts to users, supporting both instant card payouts and bank transfers.
