## Chinese version

### 1. Product introduction

#### 1. Product overview

Provides intelligent and transparent global fund Settlement solutions for cross-border Merchants, automatically converting “multi-currency revenue” into “home-currency deposits,” while optimizing cash flow through flexible Settlement strategies and reducing FX costs.

#### 2. Core pain points addressed

- **Cash flow bottlenecks**: In traditional models, Merchants must wait 7–30 days to receive cross-border funds, significantly impacting capital turnover efficiency.
- **FX black box**: No visibility into the actual FX rate used for conversion, making it impossible to accurately predict the credited amount and affecting financial planning.
- **Operational complexity**: With multi-currency revenue in USD, EUR, GBP, etc., Merchants must handle each currency separately and lack a unified view of funds.

### 2. Intelligent Settlement strategies

#### 1. Flexible Settlement cycles (Flexible Settlement)

Based on different Merchant funding needs, we provide differentiated Settlement cadences:

| Settlement mode | Cycle | Applicable scenario |
|---------|------|---------|
| **T+1 expedited Settlement** | 1 business day after the transaction | SMEs with high capital turnover requirements |
| **T+7 standard Settlement** | 7 business days after the transaction | Most cross-border Merchants |
| **Monthly Settlement** | Settled in a consolidated manner at month-end | Large enterprises, facilitating financial Reconciliation |

<Note>
Settlement cycles can be personalized based on factors such as the Merchant’s credit level and transaction history.
</Note>

#### 2. Unified multi-currency management (Multi-Currency Aggregation)

Whether your customers pay in USD, EUR, JPY, or other currencies, we can consolidate them and convert them into your designated Settlement currency.

- **Intelligent currency identification**: The system automatically identifies the transaction’s original currency and calculates the post-conversion local-currency amount in real time.
- **Per-currency reporting**: Provides dual financial reports by original currency and Settlement currency to meet different financial analysis needs.

#### 3. Holiday-aware scheduling (Holiday-Aware Scheduling)

Taking into account differences in banking holidays around the world, our system intelligently adjusts Settlement timing:

- **Holiday advance**: If the scheduled Settlement date falls on a holiday, the system automatically moves it forward to the nearest business day.
- **Cross-time-zone optimization**: Based on the time difference between the collection location and the Settlement location, selects the optimal window for fund transfer.

---

### 3. FX services and transparent pricing

#### 1. Real-time transparent FX (Real-Time Transparent FX)

- All FX conversions use real-time market mid-rates with no hidden markups.
- FX information is fully disclosed in Settlement reports, supporting Merchant self-audit.

#### 2. Smart FX execution (Smart FX Execution)

- Based on historical FX volatility models, the system automatically executes conversions in an optimal time window to reduce FX risk.
- Supports batch conversions to reduce fee overhead from single conversions.

#### 3. Advanced features: FX management tools (optional)

- **FX rate lock**: Supports locking in future Settlement FX rates in advance to eliminate FX volatility risk.
- **FX rate alerts**: Set FX thresholds and receive automatic notifications when market rates reach the target.
---

### 4. Fund security and intelligent monitoring

#### 1. Smart reserve management (Smart Reserve Management)

- Dynamically adjusts reserve ratios based on the Merchant’s historical refund and dispute rates, avoiding excessive capital lockup.
- Reserve balances are available in real time, and Merchants can apply for release in the Dashboard.

#### 2. Automated exception handling (Auto Exception Handling)

- The system monitors fund movements 24/7 and automatically triggers manual review workflows for suspicious transactions.
- Abnormal Settlement statements are automatically paused and alerts are sent to reduce potential losses.

---

### 5. Integration and API support

#### 1. Visual Dashboard

Merchants can use the Dashboard to:
- View Settlement status and fund balances in real time
- Download Settlement reports and Reconciliation statements
- Manage withdrawal accounts and withdrawal requests

#### 2. Open API integration

- Provides complete APIs for Settlement queries, withdrawal requests, and report export.
- Supports Webhook push notifications for Settlement status changes, with no polling required.

---

### FAQ

**Q: Which account will Settlement funds be paid into?**

A: Settlement funds will be paid into the bank account registered by the Merchant with Waffo. Multiple withdrawal accounts are supported, and Merchants can manage them in the Dashboard.

**Q: How do I query the Settlement status of each transaction?**

A: Merchants can query the Settlement progress of each transaction in real time via the Dashboard transaction management module or by calling the Settlement query API.

**Q: What happens when the Settlement currency differs from the transaction currency?**

A: Waffo automatically performs FX conversion using the real-time FX rate on the Settlement date. The FX rate and amount are fully disclosed in the Settlement report.
