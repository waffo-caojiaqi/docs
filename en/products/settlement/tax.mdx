## Chinese version

### I. Product introduction

#### 1. Product overview

Waffo Global Tax is an intelligent tax solution purpose-built for cross-border digital commerce. Whether your customers are in the United States where Sales Tax applies, the EU/UK where VAT (Value Added Tax) applies, or APAC markets where GST (Goods and Services Tax) applies, we can automatically identify and handle local tax requirements.

#### 2. Core value: MoR model

Using the **Merchant of Record (MoR)** model, Waffo is the seller of record for your global transactions.

| Comparison item | Traditional model | Waffo MoR model |
|--------|---------|---------------|
| **Tax registration** | Separate registration required in each country/state | Waffo holds tax qualifications in major global markets |
| **Tax calculation** | Manual processing, error-prone | Real-time automated calculation |
| **Filing responsibility** | The merchant files with each country’s tax authority | Waffo acts as the authorized agent and assumes legal liability |
| **Accounting cost** | Requires hiring local accountants | No additional accounting spend required |

#### 3. Supported global tax types

The system covers indirect tax regimes in 100+ countries and regions worldwide:

- **North America**: US Sales Tax (down to state/county/city level), Canada GST/HST/PST
- **Europe (EMEA)**: EU 27-country VAT, UK VAT, Middle East VAT
- **Asia-Pacific (APAC)**: Australia/New Zealand GST, Japan JCT, Singapore GST, and more

---

### II. Core capabilities

#### 1. Intelligent cross-border B2B vs. B2C determination

In Europe and parts of APAC, tax rules differ completely between B2B (business-to-business) and B2C (consumer) transactions.

- **Automatic validation**: The system supports buyers entering a VAT ID (VAT number) and validates its effectiveness in real time by connecting to government databases.
- **Tax-exempt handling**: For verified cross-border B2B transactions, the system automatically applies the **Reverse Charge** mechanism, exempting transaction tax in line with local compliance requirements.

#### 2. Dynamic pricing display (Inclusive vs. Exclusive)

Consumers in different regions have different pricing display expectations. The system supports automatic switching based on user IP:

- **Tax exclusive**: For the US. Display price $100, with tax added at checkout.
- **Tax inclusive**: For Europe/Japan/Korea. Display price €100 (tax included). The system automatically extracts the tax portion from the total, improving conversion rates.

#### 3. Product tax code mapping

Tax rates vary by product category (e.g., digital services, physical goods, educational content). The system includes a built-in global Tax Code library. Merchants only need to tag product categories, and the system automatically applies the correct rate—reducing tax risk caused by incorrect rates.

---

### III. Service workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Tax collection (Collection): transaction stage">Calculates and collects taxes in real time, and separately pools the tax portion from the transaction total into a dedicated tax escrow account.</Step>
  <Step title="Reconciliation and accounting">Aggregates taxable sales and tax amounts for each tax jurisdiction on a monthly basis and generates the reports required for compliant filings.</Step>
  <Step title="Tax filing (Filing): compliance stage">Waffo submits returns to national/state tax authorities on behalf of merchants and remits escrowed taxes to government accounts.</Step>
  <Step title="Settlement reports">Merchants can view detailed tax filing records and remittance receipts in the Dashboard.</Step>
</Steps>

---

### IV. Funds and security assurance

#### 1. Independent tax escrow

All collected taxes are deposited into an independent tax escrow account, fully segregated from the merchant’s operating funds, ensuring tax funds are used only for their intended purpose and are not misappropriated.

#### 2. Risk reserve and FX buffer

- Tax filings involve multi-currency conversion. The system reserves an FX buffer to prevent insufficient tax remittance caused by exchange rate fluctuations.
- A merchant reserve mechanism provides additional protection for tax compliance, ensuring taxes can still be paid normally even if the merchant account balance is insufficient.

---

### V. Integration and configuration

#### 1. Minimal integration

- No changes to your existing payment flow are required. Pass the product Tax Code and the buyer address in the API request.
- Waffo automatically completes the end-to-end process for tax rate calculation, tax collection, and filing.

#### 2. Dashboard configuration

Merchants can perform the following tax configurations via the Dashboard:
- Enable/disable Tax services for specific markets
- Configure product tax code mappings
- View tax filing history and tax payment receipts
- Download tax reports (supports import formats for accounting software)

---

### FAQ

**Q: Do I need to register for a tax number in every country?**

A: No. Under the MoR model, Waffo assumes tax compliance responsibilities in its own name, so you do not need to register tax numbers separately in each country.

**Q: How is tax rate calculation accuracy ensured?**

A: The system integrates with official tax rate databases, updates rate changes across countries/states in real time, and is reviewed by professional tax attorneys.

**Q: If there is an issue with tax filing in a market, who is responsible?**

A: As the MoR, Waffo assumes full legal responsibility for filing and remitting taxes to governments. If an incorrect filing occurs due to system issues, Waffo is responsible for handling and remediation.
