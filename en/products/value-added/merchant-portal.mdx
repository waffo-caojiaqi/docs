### II. Home overview and intelligent transaction operations

This is the "all-in-one center" for your daily work, enabling data-driven business decisions.

#### 1. Home overview: a "real-time monitor" of your business pulse

- **Dynamic dashboard**: By default, displays payment (Payments) and refund (Refunds) trends for the most recent 15 days.
- **Multi-dimensional views**: Supports aggregated views of transaction counts and amounts by currency.
- **Value**: Helps operators eliminate information lag and stay on top of daily performance fluctuations in real time; refund trend charts provide immediate alerts for anomalies so you can quickly adjust operating strategies.

#### 2. Transaction management: full-lifecycle traceability and analytics

- **Advanced search and export**: Supports multi-dimensional filtering by status, time, and payment method, and one-click export to CSV reports to meet offline analysis needs.
- **Payment analytics (Analytics)**:
  - **Success rate funnel**: Provides a complete conversion view from "order creation" → "checkout" → "success" to diagnose technical or configuration drop-offs.
  - **Channel comparison**: Compare success rates across different payment methods horizontally to optimize checkout ordering.
- **Dispute management (Disputes)**: Centrally displays credit card chargeback cases. The system provides to-do reminders and online evidence submission to guide merchants to efficiently defend their rights and recover fund losses.

#### 3. Subscription management: an "automatic steward" for recurring business

- **Lifecycle management**: Designed for SaaS or membership businesses, showing subscription agreement status, user card snapshot, and billing frequency.
- **Renewal tracking**: Trace renewal history, identify failures caused by expired cards, and support sending reminders.

---

### III. Fund settlement and account scheduling

#### 1. Settlement management: eliminate the "black box" of arrival timing

- **Settlement forecasts and calendar**: Visually displays expected incoming amounts and dates, eliminating anxiety caused by lack of fund transparency.
- **Batch reconciliation**: Displays each transaction’s settlement status by settlement batch details, and supports bulk export of reconciliation reports.
- **Multiple settlement cycle support**: Supports T+1 express settlement, T+7 standard settlement, and monthly settlement models to meet different funding needs.

#### 2. Accounts and payouts: flexible fund flows

- **Multiple withdrawal account management**: Supports binding multiple bank accounts and flexibly assigning target withdrawal accounts by currency.
- **Withdrawal requests**: Initiate withdrawal requests online and view request status in real time.
- **Balance history**: Complete account income and expense details, with filtering and export by time range.

---

### IV. Configuration, integration, and security assurance

#### 1. Environments and integration

- **Dual environment management**: Production and sandbox environments are isolated separately, enabling safe testing without affecting live operations.
- **API key configuration**: Generate and manage API keys online, with permission scope control (such as read-only and full access).
- **Webhook configuration**: Configure payment event notification endpoints, with event filtering and callback testing.

#### 2. Team permission management (RBAC)

Fine-grained role-based access control to ensure secure enterprise collaboration:

| Role | Permission scope |
|------|------------------|
| **Super admin** | All features, including account configuration and member management |
| **Finance** | Settlement queries, withdrawal requests, report export |
| **Operations** | Transaction queries, dispute management |
| **Developer** | API key management, Webhook configuration, sandbox access |
| **Read-only** | View dashboards and reports only |

---

### V. FAQ

**Q: Which languages does the Portal support?**

A: The Portal currently supports Chinese and English. More languages will be added to meet the needs of global merchants.

**Q: How do I manage multiple merchant accounts?**

A: You can switch between multiple merchant entities within the Portal without logging in again, which is convenient for agents or group merchants to centrally manage multiple merchants.

**Q: How long can historical transaction data be retained?**

A: Transaction records are retained for 13 months. Settlement and reconciliation reports are retained permanently and can be queried and exported at any time.
