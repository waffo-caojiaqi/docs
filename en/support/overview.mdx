## FAQs

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Accounts and onboarding">
    **Q: How soon can I start accepting live payments after registration?**
    
    A: Completing KYB (Merchant onboarding review) typically takes 2–5 business days. Once approved, your account will be automatically upgraded to production mode and you can accept live payments immediately.
    
    **Q: What documents are required for KYB review?**
    
    A: Typically required: business license, legal representative identity proof, articles of association or an equity/ownership structure statement, and bank account information (for Settlement). Specific requirements vary by registration country and business type.
    
    **Q: Can I test all features in the sandbox environment?**
    
    A: Yes. The sandbox supports all API features, including payments, refunds, subscriptions, and Payouts. The only difference is that no real fund flows will occur.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Payments and refunds">
    **Q: Which currencies are supported?**
    
    A: Waffo supports 60+ currencies, including major currencies such as USD, EUR, GBP, JPY, CNY, HKD, SGD, and THB. For the full supported list, please contact your Customer Success Manager.
    
    **Q: How long does it take for a refund to be credited?**
    
    A: Refund processing time varies by payment method: card refunds typically take 5–10 business days; digital wallet refunds are usually completed within 1–3 business days.
    
    **Q: How do I troubleshoot a failed payment?**
    
    A: First, check the PaymentIntent error details (`error.code` and `error.message`) and refer to [Error code lookup](/developer/tools/error-codes). If it is `card_declined`, recommend guiding the user to contact their issuing bank or use another payment method.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Technical integration">
    **Q: How can I prevent duplicate payments?**
    
    A: Use an Idempotency Key: pass a unique idempotency key when creating a PaymentIntent. Duplicate requests will return the existing object instead of creating a new one.
    
    **Q: What should I do if I didn’t receive Webhook events?**
    
    A: Check: ① whether the Webhook endpoint URL is publicly accessible; ② whether the signature is verified correctly; ③ the send records and failure reasons in the Merchant Dashboard “Webhook Logs”.
    
    **Q: Which server-side languages are supported?**
    
    A: Official SDKs are available for Node.js, Python, Go, Java, PHP, and Ruby. For other languages, you can call the REST API directly—see the [API integration guide](/developer/integration/api-integration).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
