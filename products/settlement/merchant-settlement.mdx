## 中文版

### 一、产品介绍

#### 1. 产品概览

为跨境商户提供智能化、透明化的全球资金结算解决方案，将"多币种收入"自动转化为"母币到账"，同时通过灵活的结算策略优化现金流，降低汇率成本。

#### 2. 解决的核心痛点

- **现金流断点**：传统方式下，商户需要等待 7–30 天才能收到跨境资金，严重影响资金周转效率。
- **汇率黑盒**：不知道换汇时使用的真实汇率，无法准确预测到账金额，影响财务规划。
- **操作繁琐**：面对美元、欧元、英镑等多币种收入，需要分别处理，缺乏统一的资金视图。

#### 3. 适用场景

- **跨境电商**：在全球多个市场销售，需要将各地收入统一归集到总部账户。
- **SaaS 与数字服务**：面向全球用户提供软件订阅，需要稳定的现金流支撑业务扩张。
- **内容平台**：广告收入、付费会员收入涉及多币种，需要透明的汇率换算。

### 二、智能结算策略

#### 1. 灵活结算周期 (Flexible Settlement)

我们根据不同商户的资金需求，提供差异化的结算节奏：

| 结算模式 | 周期 | 适用场景 |
|---------|------|---------|
| **T+1 快速结算** | 交易后 1 个工作日 | 资金周转要求高的中小商户 |
| **T+7 标准结算** | 交易后 7 个工作日 | 大多数跨境商户 |
| **月结模式** | 月末统一结算 | 大型企业，便于财务对账 |

<Note>
结算周期可根据商户信用等级、交易历史等因素进行个性化调整。
</Note>

#### 2. 多币种统一管理 (Multi-Currency Aggregation)

无论您的客户用美元、欧元、日元或其他货币支付，我们都能将其统一归集并换算为您指定的结算货币。

- **智能币种识别**：系统自动识别交易原币种，实时计算换汇后的本币金额。
- **分币种报表**：提供按原始币种和结算币种的双重财务报表，满足不同的财务分析需求。

#### 3. 全球节假日智能调度 (Holiday-Aware Scheduling)

考虑到全球各地的银行节假日差异，我们的系统会智能调整结算时间：

- **节假日前置**：如遇到结算日为节假日，系统自动提前至最近的工作日执行。
- **跨时区优化**：基于收款地与结算地的时差，选择最优的资金划转窗口。

### 三、外汇服务与透明定价

#### 1. 实时透明汇率 (Real-Time Transparent FX)

- 所有换汇交易均使用实时市场中间价，无隐藏加点。
- 汇率信息在结算报告中完整披露，支持商户自主审核。

#### 2. 智能换汇执行 (Smart FX Execution)

- 系统基于历史汇率波动模型，在最优时间窗口自动执行换汇，降低汇率风险。
- 支持批量换汇，减少单笔换汇的手续费损耗。

#### 3. 高级功能：汇率管理工具（可选）

- **汇率锁定**：支持提前锁定未来结算汇率，消除汇率波动风险。
- **汇率预警**：设定汇率阈值，当市场汇率达到目标价位时自动通知。

### 四、资金安全与智能监控

#### 1. 智能保证金管理 (Smart Reserve Management)

- 根据商户的历史退款率、争议率动态调整保证金比例，避免过度占压资金。
- 保证金余额实时可查，商户可在 Dashboard 自主申请释放。

#### 2. 异常交易自动处理 (Auto Exception Handling)

- 系统 7×24 小时监控资金异动，对可疑交易自动触发人工复核流程。
- 异常结算单系统自动暂停并发送告警，降低资损风险。

### 常见问题 (FAQ)

**Q: 结算资金会打入哪个账户？**

A: 结算资金将打入商户在 Waffo 注册的银行账户。支持多个提现账户，商户可在 Dashboard 自行管理。

**Q: 如何查询每笔交易的结算状态？**

A: 商户可通过 Dashboard 的交易管理模块或调用结算查询 API，实时查询每笔交易的结算进度。

**Q: 结算币种与交易币种不同时如何处理？**

A: Waffo 会自动按结算日的实时汇率进行换汇，换汇汇率和金额均会在结算报告中完整披露。

## English Version

### 1. Product Introduction

#### 1.1 Overview

Provides intelligent and transparent global fund settlement solutions for cross-border merchants, automatically converting "multi-currency revenue" into "home currency deposits" while optimizing cash flow through flexible settlement strategies and reducing FX costs.

#### 1.2 Core Pain Points Solved

- **Cash Flow Gaps**: Merchants traditionally wait 7–30 days to receive cross-border funds, severely impacting cash flow efficiency.
- **FX Black Box**: Lack of visibility into actual exchange rates used, making it impossible to accurately forecast received amounts.
- **Operational Complexity**: Managing multi-currency revenues (USD, EUR, GBP, etc.) separately without a unified fund view.

#### 1.3 Use Cases

- **Cross-border E-commerce**: Selling across multiple global markets, consolidating revenues from all regions into a headquarters account.
- **SaaS & Digital Services**: Providing software subscriptions to global users, requiring stable cash flow to support business expansion.
- **Content Platforms**: Advertising and membership revenue across multiple currencies requiring transparent FX conversion.

---

### 2. Smart Settlement Strategy

#### 2.1 Flexible Settlement Cycles

We offer differentiated settlement cadences based on merchant fund requirements:

| Settlement Mode | Cycle | Use Case |
|----------------|-------|----------|
| **T+1 Express** | 1 business day after transaction | SMEs with high cash flow requirements |
| **T+7 Standard** | 7 business days after transaction | Most cross-border merchants |
| **Monthly** | End of month consolidated | Large enterprises for easier reconciliation |

<Note>
Settlement cycles can be personalized based on merchant credit rating, transaction history, and other factors.
</Note>

#### 2.2 Multi-Currency Unified Management (Aggregation)

Regardless of whether customers pay in USD, EUR, JPY, or other currencies, we aggregate and convert all to your designated settlement currency.

- **Smart Currency Recognition**: System automatically identifies the original transaction currency and calculates the home currency amount in real time.
- **Multi-currency Reports**: Provides dual financial reports by original currency and settlement currency for diverse financial analysis needs.

#### 2.3 Holiday-Aware Scheduling

Considering banking holiday differences worldwide, our system intelligently adjusts settlement timing:

- **Holiday Advance**: If a settlement date falls on a holiday, the system automatically advances to the nearest business day.
- **Cross-timezone Optimization**: Selects the optimal fund transfer window based on time zone differences between collection and settlement locations.

#### 3.1 Real-Time Transparent FX

#### 3.3 Advanced Features: FX Management Tools (Optional)

### 6. FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

**Q: Which account will settlement funds be deposited to?**

A: Funds are deposited into the bank account registered with Waffo. Multiple withdrawal accounts are supported and can be managed in the Dashboard.

**Q: How can I check the settlement status of each transaction?**

A: Merchants can query real-time settlement progress via the Transaction Management module in Dashboard or by calling the Settlement Query API.

**Q: How are different transaction and settlement currencies handled?**

A: Waffo automatically converts currencies at the real-time exchange rate on the settlement date. The rate and converted amount are fully disclosed in the settlement report.
