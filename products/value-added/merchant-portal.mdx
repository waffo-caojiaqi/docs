### 二、首页概览与智能交易运维

这是您日常工作的"全能中心"，通过数据驱动业务决策。

#### 1. 首页概览：业务脉搏的"实时监控器"

- **动态仪表盘**：默认展示最近 15 天的收款（Payments）与退款（Refunds）趋势。
- **多维视图**：支持按不同币种汇总查看交易笔数与金额。
- **价值**：帮助经营者消除信息滞后，即时掌握每日业绩波动；通过退款趋势图第一时间警觉异常，快速调整经营策略。

#### 2. 交易管理：全链路追溯与分析

- **深度检索与导出**：支持按状态、时间、支付方式多维度筛选单据，并可一键导出 CSV 报表，满足线下分析需求。
- **支付分析 (Analytics)**：
  - **成功率漏斗**：提供从"创单" → "收银台" → "成功"的完整转化视图，诊断技术或配置流失。
  - **渠道对比**：横向对比不同支付方式的成功率，优化收银台排序。
- **争议处理 (Disputes)**：集中展示信用卡拒付案件。系统提供待办提醒及在线证据提交功能，引导商户高效维权，挽回资金损失。

#### 3. 订阅管理：周期性业务的"自动管家"

- **生命周期管理**：专为 SaaS 或会员业务设计，展示订阅协议的状态、用户卡号快照及扣款频率。
- **续费追踪**：追溯续费历史，识别因卡片过期导致的失败，辅助发送提醒。

---

### 三、资金结算与账户调度

#### 1. 结算管理：消除到账"黑盒"

- **结算预估与日历**：可视化展示未来预计到账金额与时间，消除资金不透明的焦虑。
- **批次对账**：按结算批次明细展示每笔交易的结算状态，支持批量导出对账报表。
- **多账期支持**：支持 T+1 快速结算、T+7 标准结算及月结模式，满足不同资金需求。

#### 2. 账户与付款：资金灵活流转

- **多提现账户管理**：支持绑定多个银行账户，灵活指定不同币种的提现目标账户。
- **提现申请**：在线发起提现申请，实时查看申请状态。
- **余额历史**：完整的账户收支明细，支持按时间范围筛选与导出。

---

### 四、配置集成与安全保障

#### 1. 环境与集成

- **双环境管理**：生产环境与沙箱环境独立隔离，支持安全测试而不影响线上业务。
- **API 密钥配置**：在线生成和管理 API Key，支持权限范围控制（如只读、完整访问）。
- **Webhook 配置**：配置支付事件通知地址，支持事件筛选与回调测试。

#### 2. 团队权限管理 (RBAC)

精细化的基于角色的访问控制，保障企业协作安全：

| 角色 | 权限范围 |
|------|---------|
| **超级管理员** | 全部功能，含账户配置与成员管理 |
| **财务** | 结算查询、提现申请、报表导出 |
| **运营** | 交易查询、争议处理 |
| **开发** | API 密钥管理、Webhook 配置、沙箱访问 |
| **只读** | 仅查看看板与报表 |

### 五、常见问题 (FAQ)

**Q: Portal 支持哪些语言？**

A: Portal 目前支持中文、英文，后续将陆续增加更多语言版本，以满足全球商户的使用需求。

**Q: 如何管理多个商户账号？**

A: 支持在 Portal 内切换多个商户主体，无需重新登录，方便代理商或集团商户统一管理旗下多个商户。

**Q: 历史交易数据可以保留多久？**

A: 交易记录保留 13 个月，结算与对账报表永久保留，支持随时查询与导出。

### II. Home & Operations

#### 1. Home: The "Real-time Monitor" of Business Pulse

- **Dynamic Dashboard**: Displays the last 15 days of payment and refund trends by default.
- **Multi-dimensional Views**: Summarize transaction counts and amounts by different currencies.
- **Value**: Eliminates information lag for operators, providing instant awareness of daily performance fluctuations; refund trend charts provide early warning of anomalies for rapid strategy adjustments.

#### 2. Transaction Management: Traceability & Analytics

- **Deep Search & Export**: Filter transactions by status, time, and payment method; one-click CSV export for offline analysis.
- **Payment Analytics**:
  - **Success Rate Funnel**: Complete conversion view from "order created" → "checkout" → "success" to diagnose technical or configuration drop-offs.
  - **Channel Comparison**: Side-by-side comparison of success rates across payment methods to optimize checkout ordering.
- **Dispute Management**: Centralized display of credit card chargeback cases with to-do reminders and online evidence submission, helping merchants efficiently protect their interests and recover funds.

#### 3. Subscription: The "Automatic Steward" for Recurring Business

- **Lifecycle Management**: Designed for SaaS or membership businesses, showing subscription agreement status, user card snapshots, and billing frequency.
- **Renewal Tracking**: Trace renewal history, identify card expiry failures, and support sending reminder notifications.

---

### III. Settlement & Payout

#### 1. Settlement Management: Removing the "Black Box"

- **Settlement Forecast & Calendar**: Visualize expected settlement amounts and timing, eliminating anxiety about fund transparency.
- **Batch Reconciliation**: Detailed settlement status per transaction by batch, with bulk export of reconciliation reports.
- **Multiple Cycle Support**: T+1 express settlement, T+7 standard, and monthly settlement to meet different fund requirements.

#### 2. Accounts & Payouts: Flexible Fund Flow

- **Multiple Withdrawal Accounts**: Bind multiple bank accounts and flexibly designate target withdrawal accounts per currency.
- **Withdrawal Requests**: Submit withdrawal requests online and track application status in real time.
- **Balance History**: Complete account income/expense records with time-range filtering and export.

---

### IV. Settings & Security

#### 1. Integration & Environments

- **Dual Environment Management**: Production and sandbox environments are independently isolated, enabling safe testing without affecting live operations.
- **API Key Management**: Generate and manage API keys online with permission scope control (read-only, full access, etc.).
- **Webhook Configuration**: Configure payment event notification endpoints with event filtering and callback testing.

#### 2. Team Permissions (RBAC)

Granular role-based access control for secure enterprise collaboration:

| Role | Permission Scope |
|------|----------------|
| **Super Admin** | All features including account configuration and member management |
| **Finance** | Settlement queries, withdrawal requests, report exports |
| **Operations** | Transaction queries, dispute management |
| **Developer** | API key management, Webhook configuration, sandbox access |
| **Read-only** | View dashboards and reports only |

### V. FAQ

**Q: What languages does the Portal support?**

A: The Portal currently supports Chinese and English, with more languages to be added to serve global merchants.

**Q: How do I manage multiple merchant accounts?**

A: The Portal supports switching between multiple merchant entities without re-login, making it convenient for agents or group merchants to manage multiple accounts.

**Q: How long is historical transaction data retained?**

A: Transaction records are retained for 13 months; settlement and reconciliation reports are retained permanently and can be queried and exported at any time.
